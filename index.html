FAQ

Before your appointment.

How do I book?

Choose your service and book directly through Calendly.

How long does it last?

Most spray tans last about 5 to 10 days depending on prep, skin type, and aftercare.

Do you travel?

Yes. On The Glow Tan is mobile and brings the spray tan appointment to you.

What should I do before?

Exfoliate, avoid lotion or oils, and wear loose dark clothing after your appointment.

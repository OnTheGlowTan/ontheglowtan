Deluxe Rapid Tan

$65 - Includes exfoliation and moisturizing for a longer-lasting rapid tan.

A rapid spray tan is a sunless tanning solution designed to develop color more quickly than traditional spray tans. It typically requires only 2-5 hours of processing time. This accelerated formula allows you to rinse off the tanning solution sooner, making it a convenient option for those with busy schedules and ideal for last-minute events. You can control the depth of your tan by adjusting the time before rinsing off the solution. The longer you wait to shower, the darker your tan will be. Not recommended to sleep in.
